Features

Time

Recording your time is as simple, quick and easy as using a stopwatch, just tap to start and tap again to stop. Manually create or edit time entries if you need to.

Tags

Tag your time entries with projects, tasks, activities, or anything else. View your time spent on each tag to make project time management and reporting easy.

Locations New

Create locations for your time entries so you can track the time you're spending at the office, working from home, with clients or anywhere else.

Comments

Add your own comments and notes to time entries. Comments are fully searchable so you can find that reminder when you need it.

Schedule

Enter your regular working schedule and track your progress against it. Whether you work full time, part time, or any combination of days each week.

Weekly summary

View your totals for each tag and each location every week. Any time over or under your schedule is automatically calculated.

Fast

A lightweight and fast user interface, perfect for mobile networks.

Secure

Responsive

Open source

Web first

Low profile

Stores the least amount of personal data possible, only an email address.

Control

No install
